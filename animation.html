Animation

Animation was one of the things we did as part of the Digital Technology course...
The first animation we made was an rotoscope animation of Thomas the Tank Engine on a toy track. I rotoscoped the train and added my own touch, which was the train disintegrated.
The next animation was also a rotoscope animation. This one was of an aeroplane that I recorded flying over my head in Rarotonga. I also added my own touch to this animation, which was when the plane flew into a giant mout tat ate it.
